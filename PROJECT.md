**Hotel Inventory Management : Food Waste Management**

**Summary:**

a Hotel Inventory Management Project that will create a food inventory management system for hotels to solve the food waste problem and calculate the Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) to ensure that goods can be ordered in appropriate quantities based on the raw material consumption rate. In short, the topic is "Raw material inventory management problem in the context of a hotel. The system must estimate replenishment by SKU (each raw material) using the EOQ model.

**Objectives:**

\- Calculate EOQ with AI to minimize the total inventory management cost (Total Inventory Cost).

\- Reduce waste from product deterioration, reduce inventory management costs by approximately 10-20%, and improve inventory management decision-making efficiency

\- The dashboard displays an overview of food costs and SKUs.

\- The agent plans the ordering cycles and quantities of raw materials for each SKU.

\- Increase availability to serve customers

**Requirements:**

\- A web app that uses the Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) to calculate the reorder point to ensure that goods can be ordered in appropriate quantities based on the raw material consumption rate.

\- The system must estimate replenishment by SKU (each raw material) using the EOQ model.

\- Simulation the ecosystem that Hotel uses this web app to prepare to order the supply from supplier (e.g., meat, rice, vegetables)

\- Make sure to these variables in EOQ model e.g. safety stock, lead time, quantity discounts, demand variability, seasonal demand

\- Use LLM as AI agent to analyze transactional data from POS system

\- Monitor the results of its use and make improvements over time.
